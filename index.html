01 / The Problem

The Support Death Spiral

For technical B2B SaaS companies scaling from $8M to $40M ARR, customer success is a linear cost problem. As product complexity grows, support overhead routinely consumes 8–12% of total revenue.

Off-the-shelf AI chatbots fail in these environments because they are limited to documentation retrieval (RAG). They cannot troubleshoot a failed API call, reconcile data across custom tools, or proactively protect churn risk before the customer complains. This creates a bottleneck that stifles innovation and erodes gross margins.
