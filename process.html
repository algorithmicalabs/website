The Sprint-to-Value Model

An elite engineering extension of your team. Deployment in 4–8 weeks from kickoff to full production.

Phase 1

Architectural Mapping (Week 1–2)

The engagement begins with a deep-dive industrial audit of your existing CX ecosystem. This isn't just a surface-level review; we perform a rigorous historical ticket analysis using our internal intelligence tools to identify high-density friction points, recurring technical bottlenecks, and systemic knowledge gaps.

Parallel to this, we audit your entire tool stack—including Stripe for billing, Salesforce for CRM, Linear for issue tracking, and your internal AWS or GCP deployment environments. We map every required API interaction, data model, and business rule to ensure a seamless integration layer.

Deliverables: Comprehensive Architectural Blueprint, Strategic API Mapping Document, and Segmented Permission-Level Scopes for each target Specialist Agent, ensuring zero-trust security throughout the system.

Phase 2

Swarm Engineering & Sandbox Trial (Week 3–5)

Once the architecture is solidified, we transition to the core engineering phase. We construct the custom multi-agent logic within a production-equivalent sandbox environment. This involves configuring the Leader Agent with your specific tone of voice and business rules, and engineering the Specialist Nodes for atomic data reconciliation and cross-tool orchestration.

During these weeks, we run thousands of synthetic simulations against your historical support data to refine the agents' reasoning paths. We specifically target the 70–80% autonomous resolution objective, ensuring that common technical requests are handled with 100% accuracy and zero human intervention.

Deliverables: Fully customized Multi-Agent Fleet deployment in sandbox, functional core integration links, and a comprehensive Stress-Test Performance Report.

Phase 3

Shadow Deployment & Continuous Tuning (Week 6–7)

This is the critical "Soft-Launch" phase. The system runs live in your production environment but operates in a **Shadow Mode**. Our agents process every incoming conversation in real-time and generate proposed resolutions and state-change actions, but these are routed through an internal dashboard for your team to audit.

This human-in-the-loop period allows us to perform high-fidelity fine-tuning of the **Supervisor Agent's** veto protocols and policy enforcement. We monitor live system behavior to eliminate hallucinations and confirm that every agentic action aligns perfectly with your brand's strict security and operational standards.

Deliverables: Daily Real-Time Performance Audits, Shadow Resolution Accuracy Logs, and final architectural hardening for the full production handoff.

Phase 4

Full Handoff & Sovereign Autonomy (Week 8)

The final transition to live autonomous resolution. We activate the public-facing agent interface across all channels—Slack, Discord, Email, and In-App Chat. The system takes over the primary triage and resolution path for the majority of technical support load, freeing your team to focus exclusively on high-stakes strategic initiatives.

Simultaneously, we trigger the **Proactive CX Agent**, allowing it to initiate outbound nudges and automated churn-prevention plays based on live usage signals. We conduct a final end-to-end audit to verify that the 70–80% resolution target has been achieved in the live environment.

Deliverables: Live System Activation, Final Operational Dashboard, Full System Runbooks for your engineering team, and a 6-month scale-up roadmap.

Transparent Engineering. Documented Impact.

We provide absolute oversight throughout the 8 weeks. Your internal teams focus on high-strategic initiatives while we stabilize and scale your CX infrastructure.
