03 / Delivery

8-Week Disciplined Engagement

A time-bound framework for engineering-grade deployment. Transparent progress. No open-ended scoping.

Weeks 1–2

Strategic Audit & Mapping

We begin with a comprehensive analysis of your current technology stack (e.g., Stripe, Linear, Mixpanel) and manual CX workflows. Working closely with your leadership and engineering teams, we prioritize the highest-leverage opportunities for agentic automation that directly impact NRR and ticket volume.

Weeks 3–5

Iterative Development & Integration

We build the reasoning loops and integration connectors specific to your use case. This phase involves iterative development and integration of the agent swarm directly into your staging environments. We ensure the agent architecture is robust and aligned with your existing security and operational standards.

Weeks 6–7

Rigorous Alignment & Handover

The system's knowledge base is aligned with your existing documentation, and we perform rigorous testing of resolution accuracy and brand-voice consistency. Once testing criteria are met, we provide handover training to your internal teams for full operational oversight.

Week 8

Go-Live & Performance Establishment

Final deployment to production. We establish a performance baseline based on the first 30 days of live operation, monitoring metrics like autonomous resolution rates and NRR lift. This step ensures that the system is delivering measurable, documented impact.

Transparent Engineering. Documented Impact.

We provide absolute oversight throughout the 8 weeks. Your internal teams focus on high-strategic initiatives while we stabilize and scale your CX infrastructure.
