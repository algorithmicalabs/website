Engineering Autonomy

At $8M–$40M ARR, technical scaling bottlenecks become revenue bottlenecks. The cost of linear support growth is not just financial—it is strategic.

Algorithmica Labs was founded on a singular conviction: that the most important infrastructure a high-growth B2B SaaS company can build is not another feature—it is the autonomous operational layer that makes every customer interaction compoundingly more valuable. We were built to close the gap between engineering-grade AI capability and real-world CX operations.

We don't build chatbots. We engineer bespoke, multi-agent CX Command Systems—coordinated networks of specialized AI agents that understand your revenue models, support hierarchies, product architecture, and customer health signals at a granular level. Every system we build is custom-engineered for one client and one environment. There are no templates, no off-the-shelf modules.

Our north star metric is straightforward: drive your autonomous resolution rate to 70–80% of all incoming customer conversations, while protecting and elevating the brand voice that built your company.
