Autonomous Resolution (40–70%+)

Our systems act as an intelligent filter, moving beyond generic auto-replies to provide real resolution. By coordinating multiple agents across your support channels, we autonomously resolve 40–70%+ of tier-1 and tier-2 interactions—delivering instant value to users without any human intervention.

Proactive Success Motions

Why wait for a ticket to arrive? Our agent swarms monitor real-time product usage signals from tools like Amplitude and Mixpanel. When high-priority churn indicators are detected, the system executes proactive success motions, preventing user attrition before it manifests as a support query.

Every customer interaction is a growth opportunity.
