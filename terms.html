Commercial Framework

Outcome-aligned commercial structures for enterprise-grade agentic automation. Transparency in every phase.

Engagements are structured to ensure absolute alignment with your business goals—NRR, autonomous resolution, and operational scalability.

  • Strategic Investment: Engagements typically range from $75,000 to $150,000, depending on the scope of the agentic swarm, technical complexity, and architectural depth required for your specific stack.
  • Transparent Milestones: A standard engagement structure includes a 40–50% upfront deposit to initiate project build and architectural mapping, with the remaining balance tied to the achievement of documented project milestones.
  • Performance & Alignment: Optional performance incentives are available, explicitly linked to documented improvements in your core KPIs—autonomous resolution rates, churn reduction, and net revenue retention (NRR) lift.
  • Continuous Support: Post-delivery managed service and continuous optimization retainers are available, ensuring that the system evolves alongside your product and user base.
